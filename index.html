HR Business Intelligence Notes — 2025
Recruitment efficiency
70.9% of all hires (107/151) were replacements. August peak (30 hires) reflects post-increment attrition. Dhaka drove 80.8% of all recruitment.
Workforce growth
HC grew 1,125→1,184 (+5.2%). Q3 drove most expansion. IIEC-1 (+15), NSS (+13), BBH (+8) led headcount growth.
Salary vs HC
Payroll rose 8.55% vs 5.2% HC — a 3.35pp gap. Per-capita cost up 3.2%. July increment injected +৳3.96M in one month.
Renewals & Promos
130 renewals & promotions in 2025 — significantly higher than previously tracked. January was peak month with 116 renewals, indicating a large-scale contract renewal cycle at year start.
Gender diversity
Near-equal overall (50.3% M / 49.7% F). Clinical support 76.1% female; non-clinical 73.9% male.
Branch performance
Barishal strongest growth (+8 HC). Ispahani IIEC onboarded Aug at 16 staff. Branch avg salary ৳36.8K vs main ৳62.4K.
